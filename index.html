Education, redesigned for the 80%

Learning Loop is not just another MOOC or another cohort-based edtech company.

We are rebuilding the learning experience from the ground up to make you confident in your life. We are specifically designing it for our families and relatives and friends in the middle class and lower middle class who consume 0 online courses in a year, but will learn actively if they receive more psychological safety, human attention and indentionally designed direction in their learning. If you can relate to that, then we're building Learning Loop for you.

Learning Loop is beautiful. Intrinsically fun to use. And comes with personalised learning journeys that are designed to work for you.

While Learning Loop is less than 6 months old, our team has been living and breathing this mission for over 10 years, never disconnected for a day from the problems that we are working to solve, or the audience we care to grow. We are based in Malaysia and Singapore and hire globally, and we are funded by 500Startups and all-star Southeast Asian founders.
